## Legacy platform modernization

I designed and implemented a modern infrastructure around a legacy ticketing platform that had not been built for scalability, reliability, or cloud-native operations. The solution uses Kubernetes and AWS services including S3, managed databases with automated backups, Secrets Manager, and Redis.

This architecture keeps the existing system operational while critical components are migrated incrementally. It provides a safer path to modernization without disrupting the business or requiring a risky full rewrite.

## Scalable services and mobile integrations

I developed horizontally scalable Go microservices with 100% automated test coverage to process and synchronize documents stored in S3 buckets. I also integrated payment SDKs into mobile applications and contributed to publishing and distribution workflows for Google Play and the Apple App Store.
